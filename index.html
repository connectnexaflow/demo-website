Why homeopathy

Treating the pattern, not just the flare-up.

Most treatments target the symptom you can see. Homeopathy looks one level deeper — at the pattern of triggers, stress, and sensitivity unique to you — and treats that.

It's why two patients with the same diagnosis can leave with completely different remedies, and why relief, once it arrives, tends to last.

How it works

Your Treatment Journey

Five steps, repeated and refined for as long as you need them.

  1. Consultation A focused conversation about your history, lifestyle, and current concern — in person or online.
  2. Case Analysis We map your symptom pattern and any existing reports before choosing a direction.
  3. Personalized Medicines A remedy plan selected for your case specifically — not a standard protocol for your diagnosis.
  4. Regular Monitoring Scheduled follow-ups to track your response and fine-tune the plan as needed.
  5. Long-Term Wellness The goal isn't a one-time fix — it's a steadier baseline you can maintain.

Conditions We Treat

Open any card for how we approach it, what we review, and how progress is tracked.

Skin Disorders

Eczema, psoriasis, acne and chronic skin conditions, treated by addressing internal triggers — not just the surface.

Learn more

Overview. Skin reflects what's happening inside the body. Recurring eczema, psoriasis, acne, hives or vitiligo often point to an underlying immune, digestive or hormonal pattern that topical creams don't reach.

What we review. Flare-up triggers and timing, family history, digestion and stress levels, past treatments tried.

Our approach. A case-specific remedy plan aimed at calmer skin over the medium term, alongside simple lifestyle guidance — reviewed and adjusted as your skin responds.

Hair Fall

Hair thinning, dandruff and early hair loss, assessed for the hormonal, nutritional or stress-related cause behind it.

Learn more

Overview. Hair fall is rarely just a hair problem. Thyroid shifts, iron or vitamin gaps, PCOS, postpartum changes and chronic stress are common drivers we screen for.

What we review. Pattern and pace of hair loss, scalp condition, recent health changes, diet and sleep.

Our approach. Treatment focused on the root trigger first, with realistic timelines — hair growth is slow, and we'll tell you what to expect at each stage.

Thyroid Disorders

Hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism managed alongside your existing reports and medication, not in place of them.

Learn more

Overview. Whether newly diagnosed or managing levels long-term, homeopathic care can support energy, weight and mood symptoms while you continue any prescribed thyroid medication.

What we review. Recent TSH / T3 / T4 reports, current medication, symptom pattern, family history.

Our approach. A supportive plan designed to work alongside your endocrinologist's treatment. We will never suggest stopping prescribed thyroid medication without your doctor's guidance.

Allergies

Seasonal allergies, sinusitis, skin allergies and allergic asthma, managed to reduce the frequency and severity of flare-ups.

Learn more

Overview. Recurring sneezing, blocked sinuses, itchy skin or allergic asthma usually follow a pattern tied to triggers, seasons or immunity dips.

What we review. Specific triggers, frequency and severity, past allergy tests, current inhalers or antihistamines used.

Our approach. A plan aimed at strengthening overall immune response over time, used alongside emergency medication when needed — your safety comes first.

Migraine

Recurrent migraines and tension headaches, traced back to their triggers — hormonal, dietary, postural or stress-related.

Learn more

Overview. Migraines are highly individual — two people with the same label can have completely different triggers and patterns, which is why one-size-fits-all rarely works.

What we review. Frequency, triggers, aura symptoms, sleep and screen habits, hormonal patterns.

Our approach. A personalised plan focused on reducing frequency and intensity over time, with practical trigger-management guidance you can use immediately.

Arthritis

Joint pain, stiffness and early arthritis, supported with a plan that considers mobility, diet and daily strain.

Learn more

Overview. Joint pain — osteoarthritis, rheumatoid or general stiffness — affects daily movement well before it shows up clearly on a scan.

What we review. Affected joints, morning stiffness duration, existing reports (RA factor, uric acid, X-rays), activity level.

Our approach. Treatment aimed at easing pain and improving mobility over time, paired with simple movement and dietary guidance suited to your joints.

Digestive Disorders

Acidity, bloating, IBS and irregular digestion, reviewed for the dietary and stress patterns driving them.

Learn more

Overview. Chronic acidity, bloating or irregular bowel habits are often signs of a digestive system under constant low-level stress, not one isolated issue.

What we review. Meal timing and triggers, stress levels, prior endoscopy or test results, current antacid use.

Our approach. A gut-focused plan paired with practical eating guidance, aimed at reducing dependence on antacids over time.

Children's Health

Recurrent colds, low immunity, allergies and growth concerns in children, addressed gently and without harsh medication.

Learn more

Overview. Children who fall sick often, react to common allergens, or seem to lag in growth or appetite usually have a pattern their paediatrician hasn't had time to fully unpack.

What we review. Frequency of illness, vaccination and growth history, diet, sleep and behaviour patterns.

Our approach. A gentle, easy-to-administer remedy plan, used alongside — never instead of — your child's regular paediatric care.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is homeopathy safe?

Yes, when prescribed by a qualified, registered homeopathic physician. Remedies are highly diluted and generally well-tolerated across age groups, including children, pregnant women, and elderly patients, when dosage and selection are guided correctly.

How long does treatment take to show results?

It depends on how long you've had the condition and how it responds. Acute issues (colds, minor allergies) often respond within days; chronic conditions (thyroid, arthritis, skin disorders) are typically reviewed in 4–8 week cycles, with adjustments made based on your progress.

Can I take homeopathy with other medicines?

In most cases, yes. Homeopathy is generally taken alongside conventional treatment rather than as a replacement. Never stop a prescribed medication — such as thyroid or blood-pressure medication — without first speaking to the doctor who prescribed it.

Is online consultation available?

Yes. Video and phone consultations are available for new and follow-up patients, with medicines shipped to your address. An in-person visit is recommended for the first detailed case-taking session where possible.

Do you treat children and elderly patients?

Yes, with dosage and remedy selection adjusted for age, sensitivity, and any existing conditions.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Any recent test reports, a list of current medications, and a brief note on when your symptoms started and what makes them better or worse.

Are there dietary restrictions during treatment?

Generally minimal. Specific guidance, if any, is given based on your individual case — there's no universal rule against coffee, garlic, or any single food.
